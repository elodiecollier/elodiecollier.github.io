Onchord

A social iOS app for music — log, rate, and review songs and albums, follow friends, and see what the people around you are listening to. Built solo, end-to-end, from SwiftUI to cloud backend.

Not on the App Store — Spotify's API restricts production access for individual developers, so this lives happily on my 5 closest friends' phones.

  • Fully custom SwiftUI UI/UX built from scratch — no UIKit, no third-party UI libraries, every screen designed and implemented solo
  • Rate recently played tracks from a swipeable carousel; friend activity feed surfaces ratings from people you follow in real time
  • Full music search across tracks, albums, and artists with detail views, album averages, and personal rating history
  • User profiles with rated songs, computed album scores, and a social graph of friend relationships
  • Spotify OAuth with PKCE flow and token refresh handled via TypeScript Firebase Cloud Functions
